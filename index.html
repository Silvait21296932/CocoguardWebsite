Research Gap

Identification & Classification

Current systems lack robust identification and classification of coconut diseases, leading to delayed interventions and reduced yields.

Severity Assessment

There is a gap in accurate severity assessment, making it difficult for farmers to prioritize actions and resources effectively.

Information Sharing

Limited platforms exist for real-time information sharing among stakeholders, hindering collaborative problem-solving.

Farmer Education Gaps

Many farmers lack access to up-to-date education and training on disease management and modern agricultural practices.

Research Problem & Solution

Proposed Problem

Early Detection and Management of Coconut Diseases

Current agricultural practices lack efficient, real-time systems for early detection and management of coconut diseases, resulting in significant crop losses and economic impact.

Proposed Solution

Cocoguard proposes an integrated platform utilizing IoT sensors, AI-driven analytics, and user-friendly dashboards to enable early detection, severity assessment, and collaborative management of coconut diseases.
